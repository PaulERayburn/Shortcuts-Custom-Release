How to use AI Assist
1. Paste or type text in the input area
2. Select an agent from the dropdown
3. Click Process (or Ctrl+Enter) to send to the AI
4. Review the output — type a follow-up to refine
5. Click Copy or Paste Back to use the result

Web Destinations — click any button at the bottom to copy your text to the clipboard and open that AI service in your browser. Paste your text into the conversation.

Agents — configure different AI presets in Settings. Each agent has its own provider, model, and system prompt tuned for a specific task (grammar fixes, narrative editing, formal rewrite, etc.).
