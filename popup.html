FAQ
How do I add a shortcut?
Click + Add, fill in the category, key combo, and description, then click Save.
How do runnable scripts work?
Set the action to "Run" and paste the full path to a .bat, .ps1, or .exe file as the keys. A Run button will appear next to the shortcut.
What are favorites?
Click the star on any shortcut to mark it as a favorite. Favorites appear at the top of every category view.
Where is my data stored?
All shortcuts are stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to the internet. Clearing Edge browser data will wipe your shortcuts.
How do I change the hotkeys?
Edit the KEY_* variables at the top of popup.ahk and reload the script (right-click tray icon > Reload Script).
